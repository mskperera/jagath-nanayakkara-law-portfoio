About Me

Admitted as an Attorney at Law of the Supreme Court of Sri Lanka, I bring more than 15 years of focused experience in civil, commercial, family, and appellate litigation. My practice is built on integrity, strategic thinking, clear communication, and a commitment to achieving the best possible outcome for every client.

I appear in courts across the hierarchy — from Magistrate’s Courts and Small Claims Courts to the Commercial High Court, District Courts, Court of Appeal, and Supreme Court — handling complex disputes with precision and care.

Areas of Practice

Comprehensive legal representation across all major domains of Sri Lankan law — from high-stakes constitutional matters to everyday family and small claims disputes.

Appellate & Constitutional Matters
